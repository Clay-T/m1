Iced coffee, Milkshake. Frappuccino is a trademarked brand of the corporation for a line of frozen coffee beverages. It consists of coffee or other base ingredient (e.g., strawberries and cream), blended with ice and other various ingredients, usually topped with whipped cream.

All of the coffee based frappuccinos have caffeine unless you specifically order it decaf. However, we have many creme based frappuccinos (ex: vanilla bean, strawberries and cream, double chocolate chip- the creme version of the java chip) that have no coffee at all.
